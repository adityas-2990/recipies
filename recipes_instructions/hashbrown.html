Hash Browns

Description

If you're a firm believer that potatoes make basically any
taste better, then you've come to the right place.
Because we are too — especially when it comes to breakfast
potatoes.

Choosing which type of breakfast potato to make can be
a tough decision, but if you're craving crispy potatoes that
remind you of your favorite restaurant, then you should try
this hash brown recipe. The shredded potatoes are formed
into pancake-like hash browns and lightly pan-fried for the
perfect crispy texture that will pair deliciously with any
breakfast dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium russet potatoes, shredded
  • ½ medium onion, finely chopped
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup oil for frying, or as needed
  • salt and pepper to taste
